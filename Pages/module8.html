Think of something you're good at that's related to tech or computer science. Teach the class about your special knowledge or skills. You may work individually or with a partner. Here is a template to plan your seminar.

Seminar

If you did not select a topic and date, you have been assigned a date. Please check the schedule below. You are expected to present on the posted date. As this is a culminating assessement, attendance on your presentation date is mandatory.

  • Be ready
  • Submit a print copy of your Seminar Outline and 'works cited' PRIOR to your seminar (minimum 1 day before)
  • Don't rely on a single flash drive or laptop. If you need to refer to specific files for your seminar, email them to yourself; post them on GitHub; have a backup plan and test that everything works prior to your presentation, NOT the day of!
  • Please make your presentation engaging. If you are bored preparing it, we will likely be bored by it too. If you focus only on something that you're enthusiastic about and that interests you, your audience will likely be more interested too
